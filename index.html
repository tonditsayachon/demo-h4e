    Understanding Halal Status

    Halal (Certified)

    The substance has been inspected and certified by a trusted Islamic organization.

    Halal (Fatwa)

    The substance is deemed Halal by religious ruling but has not undergone formal certification.

    Mashbooh / Haram

    The substance is ambiguous, doubtful, or forbidden. It is best to avoid it.

    Unidentified

    The substance has not yet been reviewed or classified.
